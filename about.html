About Elderly Nutrition

Our Goal


               TOPIC: Nutrition and Fitness.
          SITE NAME: 
          
          DESCRIPTION: This is a nutrition web application that is specifically designed for
          the elderly who would like to manage their health and improve their lifestyle with diet and excerise.
          
          (i)TARGETED AUDIENCE: For elderly above the age of 60 whether female or male
           workout benefiting elderly.
           specified diet for those with chronic diseases and also the healthy elderly.
           recipe available for their needs.
          
          (ii)SITE AIM: to provide the elderly with a guide that provides them with a
          flexible workout and diet plan to meet their respective
          health and fitness goals in
           improving bone density
           weight/fat loss
           improving chronic diseases etc...
         
          
          that would be possible to be followed parallelly with their
          day to day schedule.
          
          (iii)UNIQUENESS: Unlike other nutrition websites and apps that are generally set up for profit instead of supporting the users to obtain their physical objective, 
          this web app will enable users to reach their weight goals and also to keep a healthy diet
          as it provides important diets and exercise regimens for weight-loss, improving chronic diseases, and adopting healthy lifestyle.
          
          SITE DESCRIPTION: contain pages for:
          
           for workout plans for different muscle groups
           for different diet plans for chronic diseases.
           links directing to video tutorials on how to
          perform particular exercises.
           images for visual understanding by the users.
           recipe section for the user to cook healthy foods.
          q&a to provide one to one solutions.
